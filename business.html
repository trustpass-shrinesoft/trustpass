Symptom & Temperature Tracking with Daily Text Reminders

Daily symptom and temperature tracking requirements are becoming standard. We make it easy for you by giving your employees AND visitors one simple location to log symptoms. We then integrate with your temperature scanner to bring all information into one central location



Support for Access to Testing

When your employee needs a COVID-19 test, finding the lowest cost option takes time you don’t have. We make access to testing easy. Your employees can search for testing sites in our platform. For other testing support, such as on-site pop up, at home, or finding locations in rural areas, just reach out to us and we’ll guide you all the way!



Verifiable Test Results

Our team manually reviews, verifies, and adds the testing status to your employees’ profiles. With our verification system plus blockchain technology, you can have confidence and assurance that the test results have not been altered by anyone in any way, even by us at Tamarin.
